Mariachi Perla del Medio Oeste

April 18, 2019

Under the direction of Javier León, Mariachi Perla del Medio Oeste is an ensemble sponsored by the Latin American Music Center at the Indiana University Jacobs School of Music. Perla del Medio Oeste is keeping the traditions of mariachi alive with their renditions of classic mariachi standards. Consisting of 14 members, Mariachi Perla del Medio Oeste is our biggest Tiny Dorm Concert yet!

Featured Music:

"El Rey" @ 0:03

"Si Nos Dejan" @ 3:40

"Cucurrucucú" @ 6:56

"La Bruja" @ 12:20

"Jarabe" @ 16:33
