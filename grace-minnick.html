Grace Minnick

May 21, 2019

Grace is a singer-songwriter from Fort Wayne, Indiana. She accompanies her bright, quirky lyrics on the piano, guitar, and ukulele. Her folk-pop sound, containing elements of blues and jazz, showcases her classical vocal training. After winning Bloomington's Battle of the Bands-- Grace and her band released a new album entitled 'Mostly Home', and it can be found on Spotify and Apple Music.
