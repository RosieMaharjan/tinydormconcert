Off Rotation

January 29, 2019

Off Rotation is a jazz trio comprised of Jacobs School of Music students June Lee, Rocky Martin and Brendan Keller-Tuberg. After meeting while bassist, Brendan, was on exchange, the trio members began composing and creating songs together.
