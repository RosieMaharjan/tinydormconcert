OUR MISSION

Created by SPEA Arts Management students, Tiny Dorm Concerts is an intimate, dorm-filmed concert series filmed in Indiana University's Briscoe residence hall that showcases local and student musicians.

The Tiny Dorm Concert Series takes inspiration from NPR’s Tiny Desk Concert and pays homage to the series by capturing its ambiance and quality. We hold our concerts in Indiana University’s residence halls in order to utilize the campus space, creating a unique venue that incorporates student life and exceptional music into the daily lives of Hoosiers.
