Tiny Dorm Concert Artist Form

Please fill out this form in order to perform on the Tiny Dorm Concert series.

Please note the following:

This is a student-run initiative operated entirely by dedicated volunteers from across the Bloomington, IN community.

The video and audio from the session is controlled by Tiny Dorm Concerts for the purpose of archiving and sharing via social media.

Due to the unique use of dorm rooms, it is imperative that the instrumentation is as stripped down as possible (especially for drummers- a massive kit is unnecessary for the space).

This is not a paid performance.

Filling out this form does not guarantee a Tiny Dorm Concert.
