Darío Martín and Li-Jie Yu

September 17, 2019

Darío is a classical pianist from Havana, Cuba. Li-Jie Yu is a classical clarinetist from Taoyuan, Taiwan. Both artists are Masters students in Indiana University's Jacobs School of Music.
